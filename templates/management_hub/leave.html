Full Leave Policy
1. Eligibility

All full-time employees are eligible for paid leave as per their contract. Part-time and contract employees may have different entitlements.

2. Types of Leave
  • Annual Leave: For vacation or personal time off.
  • Sick Leave: For illness or medical appointments.
  • Emergency Leave: For urgent, unforeseen circumstances.
3. Application Process
  1. Submit your leave request at least 5 days in advance.
  2. Provide a valid reason and select the correct leave type.
  3. Wait for approval from your manager or HR.
4. Approval & Rejection

All leave requests are subject to approval. You will be notified via email or dashboard once your request is reviewed.

5. Special Cases

For emergencies or exceptions, contact HR directly at hr@company.com.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated. Please check this page regularly for changes.
