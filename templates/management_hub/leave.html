Leave Policy
  • Annual Leave: Must be requested at least 5 days in advance. Maximum of 20 days per year.
  • Sick Leave: Requires a medical certificate for absences longer than 2 days. Notify your manager as soon as possible.
  • Personal Leave: For urgent personal matters. Subject to manager approval.
  • Family Emergency: For immediate family emergencies. Please provide supporting documents if available.

  • Bereavement Leave: Up to 5 days for immediate family. Additional days may be granted at management discretion.
  • Other Leave: Specify the reason in your request. Subject to approval.
  • All leave requests are subject to manager and HR approval.
  • Unplanned absences should be communicated as soon as possible.
